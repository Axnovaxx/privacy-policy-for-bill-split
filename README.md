# Privacy Policy for Bill Splitter

**Last Updated: May 06, 2026**

### 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Bill Splitter**. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you may provide while using our Service. This Privacy Policy explains that our application is designed with a "Privacy by Design" approach. The Service is provided "as-is," and we ensure that your data remains entirely under your control.

### 2. Information Collection and Use
We believe in absolute data privacy.

**A. Automatic Data Collection:**
**Bill Splitter** does NOT automatically collect, store, or share any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) or sensitive user data. This includes, but is not limited to:
* Name, Phone Number, or Address.
* Precise or coarse Location Data.
* Device Identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, etc.).
* Financial records or contact lists.

All bill splitting calculations, group names, and history are processed and stored locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers.

**B. Voluntary Communication (Contact Support):**
We do not collect user data implicitly. However, if you choose to contact us via email for support, feedback, or suggestions, we will receive your email address and the content of your message.
* This action is strictly voluntary and initiated by you.
* We use your email address solely for the purpose of replying to your query.
* We do not store your email in any marketing database or share it with third parties.

### 3. Local Data Storage & Permissions
To enhance your user experience, the app stores certain data locally on your device (using Room Database & SharedPreferences). This includes:
* **Split History:** Past bill calculations and dates.
* **Saved Groups:** Names of groups (e.g., "Office", "Friends") created by you.
* **Preferences:** Currency symbols and theme settings.
* **Storage Access:** The app requires storage permission strictly to save the "Receipt Image" to your gallery when you choose to share a bill.

**Note:** This data is stored strictly within the app's private directory on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to any third party.

### 4. Offline Functionality & Third-Party Services
**Bill Splitter** primarily functions as an offline application.
* **Core Features:** The app does not require an active internet connection to perform its core calculation functions or save history.
* **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics), advertising SDKs, or any tracking pixels.
* **Google Play Services:** The only feature requiring an internet connection is the "Support Developer" section, which securely connects to Google Play Services to display and process optional donations.

### 5. In-App Purchases (Support Developer)
Our application includes optional In-App Purchases (IAP) for users who wish to support the developer. All payments and transactions are processed entirely and securely by **Google Play Billing**.
* We do NOT collect, process, or store your payment information, credit card details, or personal financial data.
* All purchase records are securely handled by your Google account.

### 6. Data Security
Since we do not automatically collect or transmit your personal calculation data over the internet, your information is safe from remote hacking or server-side data breaches. The security of your data depends on the security of your physical device.

### 7. User Control & Data Deletion
You have total control over your data:
* **Clearing Data:** You can reset all saved settings and history by going to your device's **Settings > Apps > Bill Splitter > Storage > Clear Data**.
* **Uninstalling:** Uninstalling the app will automatically and permanently delete all locally stored settings and data.

### 8. Children’s Privacy
Our Service does not target anyone under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is compliant with global privacy regulations. It is safe for users of all ages.
